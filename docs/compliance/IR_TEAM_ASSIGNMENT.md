   - Initial triage
   - Basic containment
   - IRC notification for critical incidents

2. **Level 2**: IRC (15-30 minutes)

   - Decision making
   - Team assembly

3. **Level 3**: Executive Team (30+ minutes for critical)
   - CTO / CEO notification
   - Strategic decisions
   - External communications
